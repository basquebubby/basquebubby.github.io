Click Collector

Game Description

Click the clicker to collect the clicks! Upgrade your clicker button to earn more clicks per click. Get automatic clickers to get clicks even when you’re not clicking. Try and see how many clicks you can collect and compete with your friends by showing off your score!

My Process

I was originally going to make a calculator app, but I decided to make something that I enjoyed. I decided to make a game where you click on an object and see how many things you can collect. I got my inspiration from a cookie clicker game, and decided to make a click collector. Although my game isn’t as advanced, it still has the same enjoyable effect and will keep you wanting to come back and play again.
